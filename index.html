YOUR FUNDS, YOUR WAY

Flexible withdrawal options

For on-demand access to your funds whenever you need them

Structured payout plans

For planned retirement income with regular distributions

Instant

  • Any amount, processed instantly via app (e.g., USDC, ETH)
  • Gas fees apply; reduced with Layer 2 (e.g., Polygon)

Scheduled

  • Recurring transfers (e.g., monthly), configured in app
  • Adjustable frequency or triggers
  • Automated via smart contracts with pool integration

Fixed Amount

  • Consistent amount (e.g., monthly) on a set schedule
  • Configurable terms; optional inflation adjustments
  • Funds not yet withdrawn remain invested, earning yield

Percentage-Based

  • % of balance withdrawn, recalculated periodically
  • Scales with fund growth; customizable limits
  • Subject to available liquidity in DeFi strategies

Withdrawals may vary depending on liquidity and the specific pools the DAO invests in (e.g., staking unbonding)
